Meet Your Specialist

Dr. Akshaya Narayanan

BDS, MDS — TN Govt Dental College & Hospital

Dr. Akshaya Narayanan is a Periodontist (Gum Specialist) & Implantologist with over five years of specialist-level practice. She completed her BDS and went on to earn her MDS in Periodontics from the Tamil Nadu Government Dental College & Hospital — one of the most respected dental institutions in the country.

Committed to providing the highest quality dentistry with ethics and integrity in a warm and compassionate atmosphere, her clinical approach blends precision with empathy. Whether it is a routine cleaning or a complex full-mouth rehabilitation, Dr. Akshaya brings the same level of focus, care, and transparency to every patient she sees.

She is particularly adept at managing the dental needs of elderly patients, including octogenarians, and those with complex medical histories (such as diabetic, cardiac, and oncology patients), ensuring treatments are safe, methodical, and pain-free.

She founded Sai Nivedhan Dental Care with a simple but powerful belief: every person deserves access to specialist-quality dental care in an environment that feels safe, welcoming, and honest. That belief shows in every interaction at the clinic — supported by her dedicated and caring staff, including Nurse Durga.

Languages known: Tamil, English, and Hindi.

Our Story

Born Out of a Gap in Specialist Care

When Dr. Akshaya completed her MDS in Periodontics, she noticed something striking during her postgraduate training: patients with serious gum disease, failing teeth, or complex implant needs were often turned away from general clinics — or worse, undertreated by practitioners not trained to handle their conditions.

That gap is what Sai Nivedhan Dental Care was created to fill. Founded in Mogappair West, Chennai, the clinic was built on a single principle — that specialist-quality dental care should be accessible, affordable, and delivered with genuine compassion.

Today, the clinic serves hundreds of families across Chennai. From young children getting their first dental check-up to adults undergoing full-mouth rehabilitations, every patient receives the same standard: the best care Dr. Akshaya can give.

“I started this clinic because I wanted patients to finally feel like someone was truly looking after them — not just treating a tooth, but treating a person.” — Dr. Akshaya Narayanan
